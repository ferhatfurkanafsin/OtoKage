Method 1: Delete Account Through the App

Follow these steps to delete your account directly from the OtoKage app:

1 Open OtoKage App

2 Tap the Menu Icon

Method 2: Email Request

If you're unable to access the app or prefer to delete your account via email, you can send a deletion request to us.

1 Send an Email

Send an email to mobex10@gmail.com with the subject line:

Subject: "Delete OtoKage Account"

2 Include Your Information

In the email body, please include:

  • The email address associated with your OtoKage account
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

3 Wait for Confirmation

We will process your request within 48 hours and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Timeline

  • In-app deletion: Immediate (instant deletion)
  • Email request: Within 48 hours

After Deletion

After your account is deleted:

  • You will be signed out and redirected to the sign-in screen
  • You cannot recover your data
  • You can create a new account anytime with the same or different email
  • Your search history will not be synced if you reinstall the app
