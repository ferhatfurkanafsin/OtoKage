Privacy Policy for OtoKage

Last updated: January 22, 2025

1. Introduction

OtoKage ("we", "our", or "us") operates the OtoKage mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our anime music recognition app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Email Addresses

We collect email addresses when you choose to sign up for an account. This is optional - you can use our Guest Mode without providing an email.

  • Why we collect it: For account authentication and to sync your search history across devices
  • How we store it: Securely on our backend server and locally on your device
  • Who we share it with: No third parties

2.2 Search History

We store your song search history to provide convenience features and personalized experience.

  • Guest users: History is stored locally only on your device (max 20 items)
  • Signed-in users: History is synced to our backend server (max 100 items) and cached locally
  • What we store: Song title, artist, album, search timestamp, Spotify/YouTube URLs, match score
  • Who we share it with: No third parties

2.3 Audio Recordings

Important: Audio recordings are immediately deleted after processing.

We temporarily collect audio recordings (5-15 seconds) to provide music recognition services.

  • What happens to your audio:
    1. You record audio → saved temporarily to your device
    2. Audio sent to ACRCloud (our music recognition partner) for analysis
    3. Recognition result returned to you
    4. Audio file immediately deleted from your device
    5. Audio is NOT stored on OtoKage servers
  • Third-party sharing: Audio is shared only with ACRCloud for music recognition purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Provide music recognition services
  • Display and sync your search history
  • Improve our services and user experience

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 ACRCloud (Music Recognition)

We use ACRCloud's API to identify songs from audio recordings. Your audio data is shared with ACRCloud for this purpose. Please review ACRCloud's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Google Sign-In (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google, we use Google's authentication service. Please review Google's Privacy Policy.

4.3 Render.com (Hosting)

Our backend is hosted on Render.com. User data (emails and search history) is stored on Render's servers. Please review Render's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS with TLS encryption
  • Secure hosting: Backend hosted on Render.com with enterprise-grade security
  • Access controls: Strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access

6. Data Retention

  • Audio recordings: Immediately deleted after recognition (not stored)
  • Email addresses: Retained until you delete your account
  • Search history: Retained until you clear your history or delete your account

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View your search history in the app
  • Clear your history: Delete all search history at any time via the "Clear History" button
  • Delete your account: Permanently delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Delete Account
  • Use Guest Mode: Use the app without providing any personal information

8. Children's Privacy

OtoKage is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using OtoKage, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Your Consent

By using OtoKage, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our collection and use of information as described herein.
